About Us

Welcome to N-Blade, your premier destination for high-quality folding custom knives. Founded by J. Nemeth, a master knife maker hailing from Hungary and currently residing in the picturesque south of Ireland. With a passion for knives that spans over six years, J.Nemeth has established a reputation for delivering exceptional craftsmanship and attention to detail in every blade he creates. Serving the Hungarian and European market, N-Blade is committed to providing knife enthusiasts with finely crafted, bespoke folding knives that embody the perfect blend of functionality and artistry. Explore our collection and experience the unparalleled quality and dedication that goes into each N-Blade creation.
